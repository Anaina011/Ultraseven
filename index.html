About us

Discover
Our Company

Ultra Seven Academy is a skill-first training hub focused on three fast-growing tech verticals — Drone Technology, Embedded Systems & IoT, and Cybersecurity. We believe that hands-on, mentor-guided learning is the best way to master modern tools.

In our Drone Technology program, students don’t just fly drones — they learn to build them from scratch, understand the electronics behind flight, and explore how drones are reshaping industries like defense, agriculture, and filmmaking.

Whether you're a student, graduate, or career shifter, we help you go from zero to certified through intensive workshops, bootcamps, and real-world projects.
